== Description ==
SofA is a SEO plugin where you can exchange external links betwen SofA users.  
SofA will connect you with different users having similar content and will allow you to turn into links with them. 
You can Exchange your content now and build external links network.
SofA is the biggest network where you can share your content with different people around the world. 
You can write posts which contain links to your page and add to SofA repository. 
Someone else will publish your post on then Blog and will give you external link to your page. 
SofA will find right person for you. Based on your tags and tags from another people we merge people in smaller groups. 
Every one in the group can read your post. 
When someone decide to publish your work we will remove your article from SofA repository to protect users against duplicate content issue.
Build popularity using external link network. 

== Installation ==

Upload the Sofa plugin to your blog, Activate it, then enter your [Sofa Api Key].
1, 2, 3: You're done!
